AI enabled Social Distance Monitoring with Alarm

Analytics for a Safer World with automated social distancing monitor

This product is a solution utilizes existing CCTV infrastructure to measure, monitor, predict, alert and notify of potential mass gatherings in physical venues and outdoor locations.

Key Benefits

  • Real-time measurements of volumes of people
  • Ability to set people limits for any area or zone to monitor
  • Real-time notifications and alerts when zone volume limits are approached and exceeded
  • Easy by design, to install, to configure, and to use
  • Actionable data when and where you need it with hourly and daily reports or dashboard with real-time alerts
  • Configure new zones in minutes to monitor any potential hotspots
  • Multi-platform and multi-vendor

Key Features

  • RPROVEN PLATFORM to monitor and manage crowds via existing Wi-Fi infrastructure or hotspots, both indoors and outdoors
  • REAL-TIME ACTIONABLE DATA when you need it, where you need it, with graphical real-time views and alerts
  • CONFIGURABLE to specific user/stakeholder needs and create easily new zones to monitor
  • CLOUD BASED and multi-tenancy with multi-platform and multi-vendor capabilities to combine data indoors and out
  • AEASY BY DESIGN and fast to deploy, configure, and use: Results within 1-2 days
